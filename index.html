Gameplay Controls

Player 1

  • W: Forward
  • S: Reverse
  • D: Rotate Clockwise
  • A: Rotate Anti-Clockwise
  • E: Strafe Right
  • Q: Strafe Left
  • SPACE: Fire

Player 2

  • I: Forward
  • K: Reverse
  • L: Rotate Clockwise
  • J: Rotate Anti-Clockwise
  • O: Strafe Right
  • U: Strafe Left
  • DELETE: Fire
